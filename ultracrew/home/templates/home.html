Welcome to UltraCrew!

Volunteers and crew are the heroes of ultramarathons.

We crazy ultramarathons get to spend the whole day doing something we love: running. Meanwhile, volunteers and crew sacrifice their day to make it all possible. The UltraCrew app was designed to make their job a bit eaiser, more enjoyable, and more predictable.

This app lets crew memebers:

  • See time projections based on a runner's goal
  • Easily log a runner's progress as they arrive at each aid station
  • Compare a runner's progress with their goal
  • Know when to arrive at future aid stations based on the runner's last split pace
To get started:
  1. Create an account and make sure crew members have created accounts too.
  2. Log in and create a race on the 'Add Race' page.
  3. Share the link from the 'My Races' page with your crew and anyone wants to cheer you on.
  4. On race day, the crew may log in with their accounts to log your arrival at each aid station
  5. After two checkpoints have been logged, crew will be able to see predicted arrival times for each future aid station.
